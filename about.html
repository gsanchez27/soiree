Who We Are

A Decade of Crafting Beautiful Beginnings

SoireeWeb was founded in 2015 with a simple but powerful belief: every love story deserves to be celebrated with the same care, creativity, and commitment that built it. What began as a small coordination team in Metro Manila has grown into one of the Philippines' most trusted wedding planning companies.

Over ten years and 500+ weddings later, we remain deeply passionate about what we do. From intimate ceremonies at cliffside chapels to grand ballroom celebrations in the heart of BGC — every event we coordinate carries our signature blend of elegance, warmth, and meticulous attention to detail.

We believe weddings are more than events. They are the beginning of a new chapter — and we consider it an honour to be part of that story.
