Comprehensive Wedding & Event Services

Whether you need full end-to-end planning or expert help for just your wedding day, SoireeWeb offers a service perfectly suited to your needs. Every offering is customizable, transparent, and delivered with our signature standard of excellence.

Our Wedding Services in Detail

Full Service

Full Wedding Planning

Our most comprehensive offering. From the moment you say yes to the final dance, we manage every aspect of your wedding journey — turning your vision into reality while you enjoy the engagement period with your loved ones.

  • Unlimited consultations throughout the planning journey
  • Venue sourcing, site visits, and contract negotiation
  • Budget creation, tracking, and supplier payment management
  • Full vendor management (catering, photography, flowers, music)
  • Rehearsal coordination and full wedding day management
  • Dedicated coordinator + assistant team on wedding day

Partial Planning

Already started planning but feeling overwhelmed? Our Partial Planning service steps in wherever you need us most. We review your existing arrangements and fill the gaps with our expertise.

  • Review and assessment of all existing bookings
  • Help sourcing remaining vendors and filling planning gaps
  • Budget review and optimization recommendations
  • Monthly check-in consultations
  • Final-month intensive coordination
  • Full wedding day coordination included

Day-Of

Wedding Day Coordination

You've planned your dream wedding — now let us bring it to life. Our Day-Of Coordination service ensures every moment unfolds exactly as envisioned, while you and your family focus entirely on celebrating.

  • Pre-wedding planning session (4–6 weeks prior)
  • Detailed timeline creation and vendor confirmation
  • Rehearsal attendance and direction
  • On-site coordinator + assistant from setup to send-off
  • Emergency kit and contingency management
  • Guest welcome and seating coordination
