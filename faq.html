📅 Booking & Availability

We recommend booking at least 12–18 months in advance, especially for peak season dates (November through February) and popular venues. That said, we do occasionally accommodate bookings with shorter lead times depending on our schedule. For last-minute inquiries, please contact us directly and we'll do our best to help.
Once we've had our initial consultation and you've decided to proceed, we'll send you a Service Agreement and a booking form. A retainer fee (typically 30–50% of the total package cost) is required to officially secure your date on our calendar. Dates are held on a first-come, first-served basis.
We understand that life is unpredictable. Our policy allows for one complimentary date change within 12 months, subject to availability. For cancellations, a portion of the retainer may be non-refundable to cover work already completed. Full details are outlined in our Service Agreement provided during onboarding.

📋 Coordination & Planning

We limit the number of weddings we coordinate per weekend to ensure every couple receives our full attention and best work. Each lead coordinator handles a maximum of one wedding per day. For high-demand dates, we may assign different coordinators from our team — but every couple always works with the same lead coordinator throughout their planning journey.
Yes! From our first consultation to your final dance, you'll work with the same dedicated lead coordinator. This ensures consistency, deep understanding of your vision, and a genuine relationship built on trust. Your coordinator will be your primary point of contact for everything — no passing you around between different staff members.
We maintain detailed contingency plans for every possible scenario — from sudden weather changes and vendor no-shows to wardrobe emergencies and timeline delays. Our comprehensive emergency kit covers common day-of issues. In 10+ years, we've successfully navigated unexpected challenges without couples ever knowing there was a problem. Our calm, solution-focused approach is one of our greatest strengths.

Yes! We specialize in destination weddings across the Philippines. Our most popular destinations include Palawan (El Nido, Coron), Boracay, Siargao, Cebu, Batanes, Bohol, and Tagaytay. We have established vendor networks and trusted local partners in all these locations.
Absolutely — we have extensive experience planning weddings for OFWs and Filipino diaspora couples worldwide. We conduct meetings via Zoom or Google Meet, offer virtual venue tours, digital mood boards, and detailed online planning portals. Many of our destination wedding couples never visit the venue until their actual wedding day, and everything still turns out perfectly.
Destination weddings do involve travel and accommodation costs for our coordination team, which are factored into the package quote. We're always transparent about these costs upfront. In many cases, the access to our established local vendor networks in island destinations helps offset these costs with better vendor rates and smoother logistics.
